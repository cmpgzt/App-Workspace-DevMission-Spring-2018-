About SF Green Grounds

SF Green Grounds is an online space dedicated to sharing basic information about some of San Francisco's beautiful ecological sites!

San Francisco is known for it's unique urban aesthetic. The iconic city is equally fascinating in it's biodiverse ecology, promoted by the penisula's amazingly versitle micro-climates. Check out SF Green Grounds to learn more and start exploring the city's natural sites!
